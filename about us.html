About Us

We are dedicated to providing accurate prayer times for Somalia and its regions. Our goal is to help Muslims perform their prayers on time by sending them reminders of the prayer times.

Our service offers prayer times for all 18 regions of Somalia, and we include additional prayers like Duha and Salatul Layl. We are committed to making sure every Somali Muslim has the tools they need to observe their faith with ease.
